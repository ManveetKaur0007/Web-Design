Summer Camp Program

In certain countries, a summer camp or sleepaway camp is a supervised programme for children or teens held over the summer months. Campers are children and teenagers who attend summer camp. Summer school is frequently part of a student's academic programme to allow them to catch up on work missed during the school year (summer camps can include academic work, but is not a requirement for graduation).

In a healthy, safe atmosphere, camp allows children to be physically active, creative, and skilled. It also allows youngsters to develop their social skills and form friendships. It aids in the development of good living habits and future abilities in youngsters.
